Categorical Probability

Probabilistic mapping of tercile categories (Below, Near, and Above Normal) relative to local climatology.

Precipitation Anomaly

Deviation from historical mean rainfall in mm/day. Identifies intense dry or wet spells at a 1-week lead time.

Total Physical Output

Absolute rainfall amounts in mm/day. Useful for agricultural planning and water resource management.

Percent of Normal

Current forecast as a percentage of standard seasonal expectation, highlighting relative moisture abundance.

Deep Learning Core

ET-NeuralCast utilizes a custom U-Net Generator combined with a PatchGAN Discriminator. This adversarial training setup allows the model to capture high-frequency spatial patterns often lost in traditional statistical models.

Multi-Source Data

Inputs are derived from ERA5-T (Atmospheric Pressure, SST, Humidity) and CHIRPS (High-res IR Precipitation). We fuse these datasets to bridge the gap between global atmospheric signals and local rain patterns.
