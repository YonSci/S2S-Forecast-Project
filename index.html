U-Net GAN Methodology

The system utilizes a U-Net Generator architecture specialized in image-to-image translation, fine-tuned with a PatchGAN Discriminator. This dual-model setup ensures that forecasts aren't just statistically accurate, but spatially coherent, capturing the "sharp" boundaries of rainfall events that standard models often fail to resolve.
